<p><ul><li>Constant Voltage</li><li>Constant Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED driver market is primarily divided into two types: constant voltage and constant current drivers. Constant voltage drivers supply a fixed output voltage, making them suitable for driving multiple LEDs in parallel while ensuring uniform brightness. In contrast, constant current drivers provide a steady output current, which is essential for maintaining consistent LED performance and preventing damage due to current fluctuations. Both driver types cater to different applications and requirements in lighting and electronic devices, enhancing efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
