<p><p>The SUV and Pickup Wheel Speed Sensor market comprises primarily two types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel rotation, providing precise speed information, which enhances vehicle safety and stability control. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors rely on the generation of voltage from the wheel's movement past a magnetic field, offering reliable speed measurements. Both types are crucial for advanced driver-assistance systems, ensuring optimal performance and safety in modern vehicles.</p></p>
