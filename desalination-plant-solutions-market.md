<p><p>The desalination plant solutions market includes various technologies to convert seawater or brackish water into fresh water. Reverse Osmosis (RO) uses a semipermeable membrane to separate salts and impurities, making it energy-efficient and widely adopted. Multi-Effect Distillation (MED) involves heating seawater to create steam, which is then condensed to produce fresh water, suitable for large-scale applications. Other methods, such as Electrodialysis and Vapor Compression, are also employed for specific needs, catering to diverse water scarcity challenges worldwide.</p></p>
