<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The regenerative blower market is typically categorized into single channel and multi-channel types. Single channel blowers utilize a single impeller to compress air, offering simplicity and efficiency for applications with lower flow requirements. In contrast, multi-channel blowers employ multiple impellers, providing higher airflow rates and greater versatility for more demanding industrial applications. Both types serve different pressure and flow needs, making them suitable for a range of uses, from vacuum applications to aeration in wastewater treatment.</p></p>
