<p><ul><li>Flatbed Agitator</li><li>Circular Agitator</li><li>Combination Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Platelet Agitator market comprises three main types: Flatbed Agitators, Circular Agitators, and Combination Devices. Flatbed Agitators utilize a flat surface to gently stir platelet units, promoting optimal storage conditions. Circular Agitators employ a rotating mechanism to ensure even distribution and prevent clumping. Combination Devices integrate both flat and circular agitation methods, offering flexibility for different storage needs. Each type caters to specific requirements in platelet preservation, enhancing their efficacy and shelf life in medical applications.</p></p>
