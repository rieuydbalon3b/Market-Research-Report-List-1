<p><p>The manual wine aerator market is primarily divided into two types: in-bottle wine aerators and handheld wine aerators. In-bottle wine aerators are designed to be placed directly into the wine bottle, allowing the liquid to flow through an aerating mechanism as it is poured, enhancing its flavor and aroma. Handheld wine aerators, on the other hand, are portable devices that aerate wine as it is poured from the bottle into a glass, facilitating immediate exposure to air for optimal tasting. Both types aim to improve the wine experience by enhancing oxidation.</p></p>
