<p><p>A rigid cystoscope is a specialized medical instrument used by urologists to visualize the interior of the bladder and urethra. This tool is commonly employed in procedures such as biopsies, tumor removals, and the treatment of urinary conditions.</p><p>The rigid cystoscope market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of urinary disorders, advancements in cystoscopy technology, and a rising demand for minimally invasive procedures.
