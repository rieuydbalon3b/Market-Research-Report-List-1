<p><ul><li>Double-Ended Ranging Drum Shearer</li><li>Single-Ended Ranging Drum Shearer</li><li>Single-Ended Fixed Drum Shearer</li><li>Three-Drum Shearer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Longwall systems utilize various shearer types for underground coal mining. Double-ended ranging drum shearers feature two drums, enhancing cutting efficiency by enabling cutting from both sides. Single-ended ranging drum shearers have a single drum and are suitable for narrower seams. Single-ended fixed drum shearers use a stationary drum, providing stable cutting in specific scenarios. Three-drum shearers, with three cutting drums, offer increased productivity and versatility across different coal seam conditions, making them effective for larger mining operations.</p></p>
