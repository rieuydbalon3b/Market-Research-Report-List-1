<p><p>The photo curing agent market is primarily categorized into two types: free-radical and cationic. Free-radical photo curing agents initiate polymerization through the generation of free radicals when exposed to UV light, making them suitable for various applications like coatings and inks. Conversely, cationic photo curing agents trigger polymerization through cationic mechanisms, providing advantages in certain formulations like adhesives and sealants. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing product performance and enabling rapid curing processes in industries such as automotive and electronics.</p></p>
