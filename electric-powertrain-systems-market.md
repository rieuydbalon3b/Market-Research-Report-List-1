<p><p>The electric powertrain systems market encompasses various components essential for electric vehicles. Motors and generators convert electrical energy into mechanical power and vice versa. Batteries store energy for propulsion. Power electronics controllers manage power flow and optimize performance. Converters facilitate the exchange of power between different voltages. Transmissions adjust power delivery to enhance efficiency and driveability. On-board chargers enable vehicles to recharge batteries from external power sources, ensuring seamless energy management and extending driving range.</p></p>
