<p><p>Phenolic resins for casting are thermosetting polymers widely used for their excellent mechanical and thermal properties. In the market, granular resin and flaky resin are the two primary types. Granular resin features small, uniform particles that facilitate easy mixing and processing, making it ideal for high-volume applications. Flaky resin, characterized by its sheet-like structure, offers enhanced surface finish and reduced voids in cast products. Both types are essential for diverse industries, including automotive and electronics, due to their durability and heat resistance.</p></p>
