<p><ul><li>Fixed Cutter Bits</li><li>Roller Cone Bits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil and gas drill bits market primarily includes two main types: fixed cutter bits and roller cone bits. Fixed cutter bits, often made from materials like polycrystalline diamond compact (PDC), utilize fixed blades for efficient cutting through various rock formations. Roller cone bits, on the other hand, feature rotating cones with teeth that crush the rock, making them versatile for different drilling conditions. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the geological context and drilling requirements.</p></p>
